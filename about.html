What is Blockchain?

Blockchain is a distributed ledger technology that allows data to be stored globally on thousands of servers while letting anyone on the network see everyone else's entries in real-time. This makes it difficult for one user to gain control of the network or tamper with its data. The most well-known application of blockchain technology is cryptocurrencies, such as Bitcoin and Ethereum.
