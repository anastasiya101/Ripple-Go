Our Key Features

Depending on how far you've walked/ran you claim xrp rewards with potential to integrate other activities.

After completing your workout you can play a mental puzzle to multiply your current reward by x10, x20, x50.

You can select a program/workout you want to complete and as you complete your program everyday you earn extra rewards.

As you earn XRP Rewards you can either keep the coins or buy items from our partners at discounted prices.
